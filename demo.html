Mysore Pak Recipe

A traditional South Indian sweet made with gram flour, ghee and sugar

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups gram flour (besan)
  • 2 cups ghee
  • 2 cups sugar
  • 1/2 cup water Water WAter Water

Instructions:

  1. Sift the gram flour to remove any lumps
  2. Heat ghee in a heavy bottom pan
  3. Add the sifted gram flour and roast until aromatic
  4. In another pan, prepare sugar syrup with water
  5. Mix the roasted gram flour with sugar syrup
  6. Pour remaining ghee and mix well
  7. Pour into a greased tray and let it cool

Note: The consistency of sugar syrup and timing is crucial for perfect Mysore Pak
